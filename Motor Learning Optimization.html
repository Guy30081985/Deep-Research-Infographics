Protocol: Overwriting Bad Habits

You cannot simply "stop" a bad habit. You must open the Reconsolidation Window (lasts 4-6 hours) and overwrite the trace.

1. Reactivate (The Old Way)
Deliberately perform the bad habit once. Bring it to consciousness.
2. Error Amplification
Exaggerate the mistake massively. Create a "Prediction Error" signal in the brain.
3. The Overwrite (The New Way)
Immediately execute the correct technique. The labile memory trace is updated.
4. Stabilization
Wait/Rest. Do not switch back to the old way.
